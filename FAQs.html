Frequently Asked Questions

So what is ThisApp anyway?

ThisApp allows you to support the sites and content creators you love, by donating some of your computer's unused processing power.

How am I helping?

While you're browsing the internet, ThisApp uses your donated processing power to do simple but repetitive calculations in the background. These are worth a small amount of money, which we automatically donate to the sites you select!

Is it running?

If the browser icon is colored (), then ThisApp is running and you're good to go! If it's grey (), then ThisApp is paused, and you can restart by clicking "Start mining" in the drop-down menu. ThisApp is meant to run quietly and unobtrusively in the background, and doesn't require any interaction after you pick the sites you want to support.

Is this Bitcoin?

No, though both Bitcoin and Monero are examples of digital currencies, which operate using a concept called the Blockchain. We've selected to use Monero because it's optimized for regular desktop and laptop CPUs, which allows you to have a bigger impact than in ASIC-dominated currencies like Bitcoin.

I can hear my computer fans, what's going on?

This is normal. Because your computer is doing slightly more work in the background, the fans may turn on to cool it down.
