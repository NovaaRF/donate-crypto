Frequently Asked Questions

So what is Collectiv anyway?

Collectiv allows you to support the sites and content creators you love, by donating some of your computer's unused processing power.

How am I helping?

While you're browsing the internet, Collectiv uses a little bit of your extra processing power to do simple but repetitive calculations in the background. These are worth a small amount of money, which we automatically donate to the sites you select!

Is it running?

If the browser icon is colored (), then Collectiv is running and you're good to go! If it's grey (), then Collectiv is paused, and you can restart by clicking "Resume Collectiv" in the drop-down menu. Collectiv is meant to run quietly and unobtrusively in the background, and doesn't require any interaction after you pick the sites you want to support.

Are you harvesting my browsing data?

No. We're not interested in your internet history, tracking you, or anything like that. We're just trying to make it easier to support the causes you care about.

I can hear my computer fans, what's going on?

This is normal. Because your computer is doing some extra work in the background, the fans may turn on to cool it down.
